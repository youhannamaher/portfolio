I am currently developing a Contract Lifecycle Management System for Federon Procurement Solutions, designed to centralize and digitize complex procurement workflows across multiple teams. The platform integrates external contract data into a unified system and manages the full operational lifecycle from initial discovery through internal evaluation, assignment, and submission processes.

The solution supports a structured, role-based workflow involving discovery teams, partners, specialists, and submission teams. Contracts are tracked through multiple stages, enabling clear visibility of their status, ownership, and progression. The system ensures that all actions, updates, and decisions are recorded within a centralized platform, reducing dependency on manual communication and disconnected tools.

Built using Power Apps, Power Automate, Dataverse, and Power BI, the platform provides role-based interfaces, automated workflows, and real-time dashboards to enhance operational transparency and decision-making. The ongoing vision of the system is to evolve into a fully integrated operations platform, further centralizing procurement activities, performance tracking, and reporting across the entire contract lifecycle.
