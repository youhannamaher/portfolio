I developed a Quality Tracking and Ticket Management system for Nestlé to centralize and streamline the process of reporting, tracking, and resolving operational issues across teams. The platform allows employees to submit tickets with detailed information and attachments, after which each ticket is automatically assigned a unique reference number and routed to the responsible processor through automated workflows.

The system manages the full ticket lifecycle, including issue investigation, root cause analysis, managerial review, and requester validation or escalation. All communications and actions are handled within the application, supported by automated HTML email notifications with deep linking to ensure seamless user interaction and follow-up. Scheduled reminders are also implemented to notify stakeholders when tickets remain pending beyond defined timeframes.

Built using Power Apps, Power Automate, SharePoint, and Power BI, the solution provides role-based views for requesters, processors, managers, and administrators, along with dashboards that offer real-time insights into ticket volume, resolution performance, and team activity. The system improves operational transparency, accountability, and efficiency while delivering a structured and scalable issue management process.
