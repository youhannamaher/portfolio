I developed an Employee Recognition and Nomination platform for Nestlé to digitize and streamline the process of recognizing employee achievements across the organization. The application allows users to nominate colleagues through a guided multi-step experience, capturing nominator details, nominee information, nomination context, and supporting documents in a structured and user-friendly way.

The solution features a role-based workflow involving nominators, HR teams, and management, enabling efficient review, decision-making, and communication. HR can evaluate nominations, provide feedback through threaded comments, and approve submissions, after which nominees receive automated notifications and can contribute additional content such as supporting videos. The process continues through management review, where final decisions are made across different recognition categories.

Built using Power Apps, Power Automate, and SharePoint, the platform emphasizes a modern UI/UX design, providing a smooth, engaging, and intuitive user experience. The system centralizes the entire nomination lifecycle, enhances transparency, and improves participation in employee recognition programs.
