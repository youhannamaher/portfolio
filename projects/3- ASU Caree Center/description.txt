I developed a smart registration and event management system for the ASU Career Center, designed to handle over 15,000 participants during the Employment Fair. The solution replaced manual Excel-based processes with a fully automated and centralized digital system, improving efficiency, accuracy, and real-time visibility across event operations.

Participants registered through an online form, triggering an automated workflow that generated a unique QR code and sent it via a professionally designed email. During the event, volunteers used a mobile Power Apps application to scan QR codes or search participants using identifiers such as phone number or ID, enabling fast and accurate attendance tracking. The system also supported workshop access control with capacity limitations.

An administrative interface allowed organizers to manage workshops and monitor operations through an interactive dashboard, providing real-time insights before, during, and after the event. Users could filter participant data, export results, and send targeted communications directly from the system. Built using Power Apps, Power Automate, SharePoint, Power BI, Google Sheets, and App Script, the solution significantly improved event management efficiency and enabled data-driven decision-making.
