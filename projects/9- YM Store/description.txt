I developed an internal digital store system designed to manage purchasing, inventory, and financial tracking within a centralized platform. The solution allows users to create accounts, browse available products, add items to a cart, and complete purchases directly within the application, with automated email notifications and PDF invoice generation.

The system also includes an administrative interface for managing products, updating stock levels, and monitoring customer activity. Inventory is automatically updated based on purchases, ensuring real-time stock accuracy. Administrators can also track top customers, monitor product performance, and analyze transaction data.

In addition, the application records all financial activities in an accounting-style journal, capturing incoming and outgoing transactions, payments, and revenue. Built using Power Apps, Power Automate, and Excel, the project demonstrates a complete end-to-end system covering customer management, order processing, inventory control, and financial tracking.
